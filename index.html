Dear students! You are about to begin one of the most significant times in your life, it is with pleasure that I extend to you a warm welcome to the campus of Seth Motilal (PG) College. It is situated at the heart of the Jhunjhunu city, SML`S depth of character, traditions, diversity, commitment to academic excellence, as well as it`s lush green campus contribute to making it such an exemplary academic institute. It was started with degree level college in 1958 and post graduate level in 1981.
